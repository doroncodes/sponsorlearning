About Us

The Israel Committee at YC was established to create meaningful connections between our students and the State of Israel. We believe that a strong relationship with Israel is fundamental to Jewish identity and education.

Our committee organizes a wide range of activities including educational programs, cultural events, advocacy initiatives, and celebrations of Israeli holidays and achievements. We work closely with Israeli organizations and speakers to bring authentic Israeli experiences to our campus.

Through our efforts, we aim to foster a deep understanding and appreciation of Israel's history, culture, innovations, and challenges. We provide students with opportunities to engage with Israel in ways that are meaningful, educational, and inspiring.
